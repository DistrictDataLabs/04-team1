Project Description

This project is a product of the District Data Labs Incubator Program, which helps qualified participants gain hands-on data science experience with the assistance of mentors through creating a data product. We selected our team of four individuals through our common interest in natural language processing (NLP). There were two objectives for this project: The first was to assist our mentor, Benjamin Bengfort, with creating visualizations for his PyCon talk on May 30, 2016. The second was to create a gallery of data products proposing more interesting ways, beyond the word cloud, to visualize text data. Our final product is this gallery. The tabs contain: Sentiment analysis visualizations Map of geographical entities

Data source

The dataset is a corpus of news and blog posts ingested using Baleen from the period March 3, 2016, ongoing. The latest dump we have received from Ben is from May 14, 2016. Currently, the data set contains 52k+ articles in HTML format for a size of approximately 3.6 GB. The corpus is still growing at a rate of several thousand posts per day. We have converted the HTML files to txt files for NLP in python.
