Text Summarization Though Visualization

A District Data Labs Project

This project is a product of the District Data Labs Incubator Program, which helps qualified participants gain hands-on data science experience with the assistance of mentors through creating a data product. We selected our team of four individuals through our common interest in natural language processing (NLP).

There were two objectives for this project: The first was to assist our mentor, Benjamin Bengfort, with creating visualizations for his PyCon talk on May 30, 2016. The second was to create a gallery of data products proposing more interesting ways, beyond the word cloud, to visualize text data.

Our final product is this gallery. The tabs contain:
  1. Sentiment analysis visualizations
  2. Map of geographical entities
Next steps:
  1. Network visualization through word associations
  2. Topic model (HDP)
